Osso bucco

A simple recipe for a tender beef shank cooked osso bucco-style!

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Beef shank
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil, add onion and garlic, cook for about 5 minutes
  2. Add beef shank and cook for about 5 minutes
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and cook for a further 20 minutes
